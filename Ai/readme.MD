# Problem Statement

College students and higher-study aspirants often struggle to understand what skills, projects, internships, certifications, and milestones are required to achieve their career goals.

Although information is available across multiple platforms, there is no centralized system that evaluates a student's current profile, identifies skill gaps, tracks progress, and generates a personalized roadmap toward a specific goal such as securing internships, obtaining a software engineering role, becoming an AI engineer, or pursuing a Master's degree abroad.

PathPilot AI aims to solve this problem by acting as an intelligent career planning and readiness assessment platform.

---

# Vision

To create an AI-powered digital career companion that helps students make informed decisions, track their growth, and systematically prepare for internships, jobs, and higher studies.

# System Workflow

1. User creates profile.
2. User selects career or higher-study goal.
3. System evaluates profile.
4. Readiness score is calculated.
5. Missing skills and requirements are identified.
6. Personalized roadmap is generated.
7. Progress is tracked continuously.
